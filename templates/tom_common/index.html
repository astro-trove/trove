Welcome to the TROVE!


The Tool for Rapid Object Vetting and Examination (TROVE) is a web application and application programming interface (API) designed for real-time vetting and scoring of electromagnetic (EM) transients, with the ultimate goal of optimizing follow-up of poorly localized astronomical events like gravitational waves (GWs).

{% comment %}
TROVE vets and scores candidate counterparts based on their position in a localization region, association with potential host galaxies, association with point sources, minor planets, or AGN, and photometric behavior. Candidates are ranked based on their score. High-scoring candidates are prime targets for photometric and spectroscopic follow-up!
